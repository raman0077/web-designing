The Budget IPhone

The iPhone SE has an aluminum frame and glass front and back. The phone's dimensions and external design are nearly identical to that of the iPhone 8 except for a centered Apple logo and no iPhone branding. Like its predecessor, the iPhone 8, the second-generation iPhone SE has the same top and bottom bezels on the front and a home button integrated with the same second-generation Touch ID (built into the Home button) found in the iPhone 8.
